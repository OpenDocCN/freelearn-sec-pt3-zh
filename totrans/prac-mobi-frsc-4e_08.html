Section 2: Android Forensics

This section will cover everything you need to know about forensics on Android devices. We will start by understanding the Android platform and its filesystem and then cover the topics of setup, acquisition/extraction, and recovery. We will also look at Android malware and how to reverse engineer Android apps using open source tools.

This chapter consists of the following chapters:

  • Chapter 7, Understanding Android
  • Chapter 8, Android Forensic Setup and Pre-Data Extraction Techniques
  • Chapter 9, Android Data Extraction Techniques
  • Chapter 10, Android Data Analysis and Recovery
  • Chapter 11, Android App Analysis, Malware, and Reverse Engineering
